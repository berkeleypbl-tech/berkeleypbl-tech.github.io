Setup Instructions

Setup Steps:

I. One person visits the Cub-E located on the left side of the GBC building, if you’re facing GBC from Sproul Plaza. It’s a little nook tucked into the side of the building.

1. You will need to provide your Cal ID to the attendant in order to check out one table, and two to four chairs.

2. Set these up immediately, preferably near the Bancroft side of Sproul, in order to reserve a spot on Sproul.

II. At least one officer and another member will need to visit Hearst Gym Cages.

1. Enter on the side facing Hearst Annex (which looks like a Trailer Park).

a. The entrance has two doors on the sides and stairs if you were to go straight.
b. You will enter to the right, when facing the building.

2. Follow the hallway down. You will take a slight left turn one time, and keep walking until you see the door to room 16. The door closest to the entrance is usually open, but if you end up needing the door code (keypad), it should be 073126. 
3. Find cage 12.
4. The officer should unlock the cage because he or she has the combination. 
5. Retrieve the wagon containing tablecloth, tent, retractable banner, and the PBL [IN]box.
6. Find our table on Sproul Plaza, and set up the tent and tablecloth.
